DIPLOMA:---

  
 Diploma Courses after 10th are short-term courses which teach the skills applicable to the real world rather than the things that a conventional degree teaches. 
  Candidates can pursue the diploma courses after 10th in various fields like Business, Management, Marketing, Computer Application, etc.
  The average starting salary range for holders of a diploma degree is between 3.4 LPA and 4.7 LPA at reputable organizations. 
  In India, diploma courses are available in a number of specializations including engineering, computer science, hospitality, management, etc.
  
  Course Level:--Diploma
  Duration:--3 years
  Examination Type:--Semester type
  Eligibility	10th examinations
  Admission Process	Merit-based or Entrance-based
  Top recruiting organizations	HP Enterprise, Mentor Graphics, Raana Semiconductors Private Limited, Techabyte Private Limited, TATA Power Strategic Engineering Division, Spider focus Solutions
  Top recruiting areas	Electrical and electronic field, IT sectors, Colleges, and Universities, Communication and Media, Automobiles, Media, Advertising, Telecom Services


  

 Diploma in Civil Engineering After 10th:


Diploma in Civil Engineering is a 3-year-long diploma programme for candidates who have completed their Class 10 and want to pursue civil engineering as a career. 
  It is a reputable diploma programme that offers several job opportunities to young students. 
  The Diploma in Civil Engineering fees vary between Rs. 50,000 and Rs. 5,00,000. 



Diploma in Automobile Engineering After 10th:


Diploma in Automobile Engineering is a full-time certificate program of duration three years. 
  It is a diploma-level Automobile engineering course, the sub-branch of Mechanical Engineering. 
  Candidates for the Diploma in Automobile Engineering course must have completed class 10th with
   a minimum of 60% marks and mathematics and Science as critical subjects of study from a recognized board.
  

  
 Diploma Courses After 10th In Computer Science And Engineering:


A diploma in computer science engineering provides a basic understanding of computer science, engineering concepts, and mathematical techniques. 
  The course lasts for three years and combines both theoretical and practical concepts.
   After completing this course, students can find job offers from research, communication, medical, and IT firms.
  

  
Diploma in Pharmacy after 10th:

  
The diploma in Pharmacy course is three years long. After the 10th, certificates or practising courses are available for the students.
   12th Science passed students are eligible to pursue this course. To secure admission into reputed colleges, one must crack relevant Diploma entrance tests.

 Diploma Course in Electrical Engineering After 10th:

  
A diploma in Electrical Engineering is a three-year engineering programme that teaches students subjects such as Electrical Circuit Theory, 
  Electronic Devices and Circuits, Transducers and Signal Conditioners, Power Systems, etc. Students can pursue it after completing class 10.
   The course deals with installation, maintenance, troubleshooting, magnetism, control systems, signal processing and telecommunications. 
   The eligibility for Diploma in Electrical Engineering courses is a minimum score of 50% marks in class 10. A relaxation of 5% is provided to the 
   candidate who belongs to reserved categories.

  
  
Diploma In Mechanical Engineering After 10th Courses: 
     
  
A diploma in Mechanical Engineering is a three-year polytechnic course that students can pursue after class 10.
   A certificate in Mechanical Engineering deals with designing, manufacturing and maintaining mechanical equipment by applying the basic principles of mechanics.
    Mechanical Diploma or Mechanical Engineering polytechnic courses are offered to the candidates immediately after their 10th or equivalent exam. 
    The eligibility for Mechanical diploma courses is a minimum score of 55% marks in class 10. 
    A relaxation of 5% is provided to the candidates in the reserved categories.
  
  Duration of Diploma in Mechanical Engineering 	3 Year
  Eligibility Criteria 	Students must clear the 10th standard with 55% marks from a reputable school.
  Admission Procedure 	Admission will be done based on entrance examination marks or Merit List.
  Expectation Salary 	20-30k 
  Job position 	Trainee, Mechanical Designer, Lecturer & Professor, Assist. Manager, Mechanical Engineer, Consultant Mechanical, Site Mechanical Engineer.


  
Diploma After 10th In Electrical & Electronics Engineering:

  
An Electrical and Electronics Engineering diploma is a 3-year full-time diploma-level engineering course.
   Candidates who have completed the 10th examination with Mathematics and Science stream or an equivalent examination from a recognized board 
   with 55% marks are eligible for Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering course. 
   Most colleges and universities offer Diplomas in Electronics Engineering courses. Every college and university has different eligibility criteria,
   admission processes, fee structure, and syllabi for this course.
